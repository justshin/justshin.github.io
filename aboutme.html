Hi, my name is Justin Shin. I am a 4th year at UC
Berkeley majoring in Data Science (emphasis in Business)
and obtaining a Certifcation of Enteurpeurship and Technology.
I enjoy working on projects with customer/marketing data
and have strong interests in working for tech or healthcare.
A fun fact about me is that having grown up moving 14 times and
living in 4 different countries, I had the privilege to be a part of
multiple communities/cultures and am forunate to make many
valuable connections throughout my life.
You can check out my photography tab to see some places I
have visited/lived in.

Besides studying data science, I have a strong interest
in web designing. This website was made extensively with
my knowledge in front-end web development.
Currently, I am interning at Samsung Electronics for the
upcoming summer working with customer/market data.
I have worked as a student advisor at the UC Berkeley
Office of Undergraduate Admissions assisting incoming
students/parents and specializing in customer service.
I am also a member of the Data Analytics Team for the
UC Berkeley Division of Data Science. Through this
organization, I am exposed to working on various projects
of my interests and a network of people who are also interested
in working with data.

Most of my volunteer/community work focuses on the
environment and helping children. Since high school, I have worked
on amazing projects in serving the many communities I lived in as
well as working as a volunteer for children programs.
My most recent volunteer work is with the Berkeley
Project, which is a non-profit organization at UC
Berkeley that serves/cleans the Berkeley Community every semester.
I have worked as a site leader numerous times for the program
since freshman year. I have also spent time during
school breaks helping out at the American Cross Association working
with government contractors/employees on military bases in roles
related to data in healthcare. Volunteering allows me another
opportunity to give back to society besides the work I conduct with data
and gives more meaning to my life knowing I am making a difference
with/for others.

During my free time, I like to spend time
with my family/friends, work out, play basketball
for my club team, and travel.
I also dabble a bit in stock trading analysis
and enjoy learning more about real estate
with whatever time I have left in the day.
