Hi, my name is Justin Shin! I'm a current 2nd year at UC
Berkeley double majoring in Data Science and Economics.
Having grown up living in roughly 12 states and 3
different countries,I have been exposed to many different
cultures and valuable connections throughout my life.
Moving almost every year was one of the main reasons why I
appreciate traveling and photography(check out
photography to see more!).

Besides studying data science and economics,
I have strong interests in web design and mobile app
development. This website was made extensively with my
knowledge in html,css, and javascript.
Currently, I am working as a student
advisor at the UC Berkeley Office of Undergraduate
Admissions assisting incoming students/parents one-on-one
and specializing in customer service. I am also working as
a freelance web developer/designer and in the process
of making a brand new social media platform.

Most of my volunteer/community work focus on the
environment and children. Since high school, I have worked
on countless projects regarding serving communities as
well as working as a volunteer for children programs.
Currently, I have been working with the Berkeley
Project, which is a non-profit organization at UC
Berkeley that serves the Berkeley community every
semester. I have worked as a site leader (leading over
several dozen volunteers) for the program since coming in as
a freshman and plan on continuing this tradition throughout
my undergraduate year.

I am a member of the Data Science Society
at UC Berkeley. Through this club, I am exposed
to various projects as well as a network of
connections of people who are also interested in
working with data science.
