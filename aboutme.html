Hi, my name is Justin Shin! I'm a current 2nd year at UC
Berkeley majoring in Data Science (emphasis in Economics)
and minoring in Computer Science.
Having grown up living in 12 states and 3
different countries, I had the privilege to be a part of
multiple communities/cultures and enjoyed making many
valuable connections throughout my life.
Moving almost every year was one of the main reasons why I
appreciate traveling and photography.

Besides studying data science, I have a strong interest
in web designing. This website was made extensively with
my knowledge in front-end web development. Currently,
I have been working as a student advisor at the UC Berkeley
Office of Undergraduate Admissions assisting incoming
students/parents and specializing in customer service.
I am also a member of the Data Analytics Team for the
UC Berkeley Division of Data Science. Through this
organization, I am exposed to working on various projects
of my interests and a network of people who are also interested
in working with data.

Most of my volunteer/community work focuses on the
environment and children. Since high school, I have worked
on countless projects in serving communities as
well as working as a volunteer for children programs.
Currently, I have been working with the Berkeley
Project, which is a non-profit organization at UC
Berkeley that serves the Berkeley community every
semester. I have worked as a site leader for the program
since freshman year. I have also spend much of time during
breaks helping out at the American Cross Association working
with government contractors/employees on military bases in roles
related to data in healthcare.

During my free time in college, I spend a
lot of time either working out or playing
basketball for my club team.
I also dabble a bit in trading analysis
with whatever time I have left in the day.
