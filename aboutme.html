A Bit About Me...

current 2nd year at UC Berkeley majoring in Data Science and
Economics. Ever since I was young, I always knew that the my
life was very different from others around me. Living in over 12

out unique foods from around the world. Check out my
blog/photography to learn more about my travels or click on my
resume/projects to see some of the projects I have worked on
during my professional academic career!
