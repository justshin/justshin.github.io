Hi, my name is Justin Shin. I am a 4th year at UC
Berkeley majoring in Data Science (emphasis in Business)
and obtaining a Certifcation of Enteurpeurship and Technology.
I have a strong interest in working for the tech or healthcare
industry. A fun fact about me is that having grown up moving
14 times and living in 4 different countries, I had the
privilege to be a part of numerous communities/cultures and am
forunate to have met many valuable connections throughout my life.
Check out my photography tab to see some of the places I
have visited/lived in.

Besides studying data science, I have a strong interest
in web designing. This website was made extensively with
my knowledge in front-end web development.
For this upcoming summer, I will be interning at Samsung Electronics
working with customer/market data insight.
I have previously worked as a student advisor at the UC Berkeley
Office of Undergraduate Admissions assisting incoming
students/parents and specializing in customer service.
I am also a member of the Data Analytics Team for the
UC Berkeley Division of Data Science. Through this
organization, I am exposed to working on various projects
of my interests and a network of peers who also have a passion
for data science.

Most of my volunteer/community work focuses on helping the
environment and children. Since high school, I have worked
on several projects serving the many communities I lived in as
well as working as a volunteer for children educational programs.
My most recent volunteer work is with the Berkeley
Project, which is a non-profit organization at UC
Berkeley that assist the Berkeley Community every semester.
I have worked as a site leader numerous times for the program
since freshman year. I have also spent time during
school breaks helping out at the American Cross Association working
with government contractors/employees on a military base in roles
related to data in healthcare.

During my free time, I like to spend time
with my family/friends, working out, playing basketball
for my club team, and traveling. I am currently in the process
of learning/obtaining an AWS Solutions Architect Certification in
my spare time as well. I also dabble a bit in stock
trading analysis and enjoy learning about
real estate with whatever time I have left in the day.
